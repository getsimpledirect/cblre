The **evaluation data** (item bank, gold answers, scoring splits) is subject to a separate license from the code in this repository.

## Code license

## Item data license (forthcoming with gated release)

The CBLRE item bank includes items from two source categories:

**OGL-Canada items.** Items derived from Canadian federal or provincial open-government materials (statutes, regulations, court decisions published under open-government terms) are made available under the [Open Government Licence – Canada](https://open.canada.ca/en/open-government-licence-canada) (OGL-Canada 2.0).

**SimpleDirect®-authored items.** Original items authored by Alpine Pacific Trading Inc. are released under terms specified in the full data release agreement. Use requires acceptance of those terms at access-request time.

The full data license document will be included with the gated item bank release. It will specify:
- Permitted uses (research, evaluation, benchmarking)
- Prohibited uses (training data for commercial products without separate agreement)
- Attribution requirements
- Redistribution restrictions

## Synthetic sample

The items in `data/sample/` are illustrative synthetic content carrying no legal or factual authority. They are released under the same Apache 2.0 terms as the rest of the code in this repository and carry no separate data-license restrictions.
